Tip 01
Constraints

Define what's
off-limits
before anything else

Before opening Figma, write down every constraint. Technical limits, brand rules, timeline, user expectations. These aren't blockers — they're the shape of your solution.

List non-negotiables first. Platform constraints, accessibility rules, brand system.

Find the creative surface. The gap between constraints is where great design lives.
