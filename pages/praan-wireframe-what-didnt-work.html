✕
No narrative hierarchy
Every page and every navigation item carries equal visual weight. The visitor has no cue about what to read first, what matters most, or where the site is trying to take them.
✕
Specs before trust
Leading with 99.97% and HEPA Grade A forces a sceptical buyer to evaluate claims before they have any context or reason to care. The conversation starts at proof, not at the problem.
✕
No audience owns the primary narrative
Three audiences — operators, investors, engineers — arrive at the same spec-dense front page with nothing specific for any of them. The site simultaneously tries to speak to all three and fully serves none.
✕
Technology without demonstration
The mechanism is filterless — the only genuinely differentiated claim. But a static photo and a data table cannot explain an invisible electrostatic process. The one thing worth showing is described instead of shown.
