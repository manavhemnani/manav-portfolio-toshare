Hub-and-Spoke Architecture

Homepage as strategic router, not funnel entry. Each page annotated with the IA principle it primarily activates.

Hub-and-Spoke model: the Homepage acts as a dispatcher — it reads visitor intent and routes them to the relevant spoke. Each spoke page is self-contained and can serve as a direct entry point via SEO, referral, or LinkedIn share. Dashed curves indicate lateral navigation users take without returning to the hub.
