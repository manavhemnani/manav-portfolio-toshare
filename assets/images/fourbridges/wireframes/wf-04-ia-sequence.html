Audience Segment Test — Sequence Performance
Visitor Type Credentials-First sequence Philosophy-First sequence Outcome
Warm referral
High trust, needs validation		 Credentials land but feel expected. No early signal of what makes this firm different. Scrolls for differentiation, may not find it. Philosophy confirmed quickly. Credentials validate. Moves rapidly to services. High engagement on first call. Philosophy-first ✓
Cold prospect
Skeptical, needs education		 Credentials before belief = "another advisory firm." No reason to trust the specific thesis. Disengages before services section. Philosophy section creates a stop. "I've never seen a firm say this explicitly." Reads carefully. Credentials confirm. Services become relevant. Philosophy-first ✓
Sector specialist
Knows the market, comparing		 Credentials match competitors. Process reads as standard. Nothing memorable. Uses persistent nav to skip to Services directly. Outcome-first cards immediately differentiate. Reaches Engage section with high intent. Non-linear nav ✓
Why credentials-first fails Opening with credentials requires the visitor to supply their own reason to care. The implicit question — "so what?" — is unanswered until the services section, but most skeptical visitors disengage before reaching it. The credentials-first sequence had no segment that moved all three visitor types through to services engagement.
Why philosophy-first works for all segments Warm visitors move past the philosophy section quickly — it confirms what they already suspected. Skeptical visitors stop at the philosophy section and read carefully — it gives them a reason to continue. The persistent nav means sector specialists bypass any section and use the site as a reference tool. The philosophy-first sequence had no segment that disengaged before reaching the services section.
How this translated to final UI
Homepage hero leads with a philosophy statement, not credentials or a tagline
Persistent sticky nav allows skip-ahead at any scroll depth
Each section designed as a standalone entry point for direct-URL visitors
"We Are Selective" placed at position 4, just before Engage — filters only at highest intent
What this decision improves
Warm visitors: Faster path to confirmation — less friction to first call
Cold visitors: Philosophy creates an information stop before bounce
Specialists: Non-linear nav respects their prior knowledge
Overall: No audience segment disengages before reaching Services
